# [Right Angle Triangle](https://www.geeksforgeeks.org/problems/right-angle-triangle-1605685807/1)
## Easy
Given an integer&nbsp;s. Write a program to print the Right angle triangle wall. The length&nbsp;of&nbsp;perpendicular and base&nbsp;is s.&nbsp;Note: Print exactly single " " after "*". Print a new line after printing the triangle.
Example:  
Input: s = 4
Output:
* * * * * * * * * * Explanation: Length of perpendicular and base of triangle is 4 .
Input: s = 3
Output:
* * * * * * Explanation: Length of perpendicular and base of triangle is 3 .
Constraints:1 ≤ s ≤ 10
