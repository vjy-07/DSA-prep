# [Butterfly Pattern](https://www.geeksforgeeks.org/problems/butterfly-pattern/1)
## Easy
Given a number&nbsp;n. Print Butterfly Pattern with n lines.&nbsp;
Examples:
Input: n = 4
Output:
*     * 
**   ** 
*** *** 
******* 
*** *** 
**   ** 
*     * 
Input: n = 5 Output: 
*       * 
**     ** 
***   *** 
**** **** 
********* 
**** **** 
***   *** 
**     ** 
*       *
Constraints:1&nbsp;≤&nbsp;n&nbsp;≤ 100
